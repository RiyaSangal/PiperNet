Inside the network

It’s not hard to see how Decentra is a truly revolutionary concept

Decentralization

Instead of everyone’s data being held on huge central servers owned by a large organization, Decentra exists on independently run servers (“pods”) all over the world. You choose which pod to register with, and you can then connect seamlessly with the community worldwide.

Freedom

You can be whoever you want to be in Decentra. Unlike some networks, you don’t have to use your real identity. You can interact with people in whatever way you choose. The only limit is your imagination.

Privacy

In Decentra you own your data. You don’t sign over rights to a corporation or other companies who could use it. In addition, you choose who sees what you share, using Aspects. With this, your friends, your habits, and your content is your business ... not ours!
